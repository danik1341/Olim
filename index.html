Phone and Internet

Mobile Phone Operators and Plans

    Cellcom -

  • Package: Cellcom offers various packages suitable for different needs, including voice, data, and international calling options.
  • Pricing: Prices start at 100 NIS per month for a basic package, with additional features and data options available at higher price points.

    • Pelephone -

  • Package: Pelephone provides comprehensive voice and data packages, along with additional services such as international calling and roaming options.
  • Pricing: Prices start at 90 NIS per month for a basic package, with customizable options available for higher usage and additional features.

    • Parnter -

  • Package: Partner offers a range of voice and data packages tailored to different user preferences, including unlimited calling plans and data add-ons.
  • Pricing: Prices start at 80 NIS per month for a basic package, with prices varying based on the selected plan and additional features.

Internet Service Providers

    Bezeq -

  • Package: Bezeq provides various internet packages with different speeds and data allowances, including both wired and wireless connections.
  • Pricing: Prices start at 100 NIS per month for a basic internet plan, with higher speeds and unlimited data options available at higher price points.

    • HOT -

  • Package: HOT offers high-speed internet packages suitable for both residential and business users, with additional services such as Wi-Fi and TV bundles.
  • Pricing: Prices start at 120 NIS per month for a basic internet plan, with pricing varying based on the selected speed and additional services.

    • Partner -

  • Package: Partner provides internet services with different speed options and bundles, including combined packages with TV and phone services.
  • Pricing: Prices start at 110 NIS per month for a basic internet plan, with prices varying based on the selected speed and bundle options.

Comparing Packages and Prices

It is recommended to compare the packages and prices offered by different phone companies and internet service providers to find the best option that suits your needs. Prices and packages may vary over time, so it's advisable to visit the respective companies' websites or contact their customer service for up-to-date information on current packages, pricing, and any special offers.
